Biography

I am a postdoc working with Prof. Henri Lorach on building a foundational model for neurostimulation, combined with active learning approaches, for personalized neurostimulation.

I did my PhD at EPFL in the Laboratory for Information and Inference Systems (LIONS) led by Prof. Volkan Cevher and Neurorestore (.NR) center led by Prof. Grégoire Courtine and Prof. Jocelyne Bloch. My main responsabilities were regarding automating the search of stimulation protocols used in the project of .NR where we deal with spinal cord injury patients, by employing intelligent decision making systems.

Until now, my academic journey was definitly not a straight path. My background involves neuroscience, engineering and computer science. I love to understand how things work and see how deep the rabbit hole is!
